Bronze Blossom
11/16/2025
Blurryface
Beautiful irl, super durable/strong. Size medium is good for small nail beds

I went with these in medium even though I have pretty small nail beds and very petite hands (ring size 4). I figured I could file them down if they were too big whereas I couldn't make them wider or bigger if they were too small and squeezed my nails too tight. Size medium is perfect so I'm glad I went with it. These aren't too wide or two narrow and the curvature is relatively comfortable if you use glue (the stickers take up a little more space under the press-on and may cause these to squeeze your nail bed uncomfortably).These are thick due to the raised design but they aren't over the top thick or so long, you become disabled wearing them. I was afraid they might look TOO wild irl because I tend to opt for more conservative nails since I don't exactly want people staring at my nails or be unable to do basic functions. Fortunately, the color theme and overall shape save these from being ostentatious or wicked looking. They are truly phenomenal and I almost don't want to wear them with glue just so I can keep them new longer. However, they are super resilient and not thin or flimsy so they are up to the task of being cleaned of dried glue on the underside for reuse.Overall, these are a worthwhile investment to me and so fun to wear because they look hand done and not like press-ons. And they are strong enough to last.
